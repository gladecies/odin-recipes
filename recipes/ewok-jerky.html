Ewok Jerky

Background Information

Served only at the Power Sliders restaurant in Pons Ora.

  • A popular Outer Rim delicacy!
  • Served only on the planet of Abafar!
  • It's a real thing! See this

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh Ewok
  • Chef Borkus' special sauce

Steps

  1. Slice the meat
    • Between 1/8 and 1/4 inches is ideal.
  2. Prepare Chef Borkus' special sauce (its ingredients are kept secret for security purposes)
  3. Marinate the meat in the sauce
  4. Dry it out
    • Arrange the meat properly on a tray
    • Bake in a star-powered oven for 4-6 hours
  5. Try it out: if it is chewie;) but still tender you're good to go!
