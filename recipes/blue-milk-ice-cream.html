Blue Milk Ice Cream

Background Information

A popular dessert for all ages and sentients. Even non-sentients enjoy this wonder!

  • Blue milk comes from female banthas, so it is also known as Bantha milk.
  • Especially popular among the Chiss and desert farmers on Tatooine.
  • This recipe is targeted especially to our desert dwellers—anyone would enjoy something cold in that hot weather.
