Blue Milk Ice Cream

Background Information

A popular dessert for all ages and sentients. Even non-sentients enjoy this wonder!

  • Blue milk comes from female banthas, so it is also known as Bantha milk.
  • Especially popular among the Chiss and desert farmers on Tatooine.
  • This recipe is targeted especially to our desert dwellers—anyone would enjoy something cold in that hot weather.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup blue/bantha milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 6 tbsp sea salt
  • 2 cups of ice
  • Patitite-sized spacelock bag
  • Jawa-sized spacelock bag

Steps

  1. Put the blue milk and sugar inside the Patitite-sized bag.
  2. Put the sea salt and ice in the Jawa-sized bag.
  3. Place the smaller bag inside the larger one and seal both.
    • Make sure to seal BOTH bags. You don't want salty ice cream.
  4. Shake the bag vigorously for 5 minutes. A droid can be used for this purpose.
  5. Place your newly made blue milk ice cream in a bowl or cone (your choice)
  6. Enjoy!
