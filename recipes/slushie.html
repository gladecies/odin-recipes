Slushies!

Description

Now you don't even need to go to the gas station to indulge in your guilty summer pleasure.

Thanks to Seeded at the Table

's easy recipe, you can enjoy slushies from the privacy of your home.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. club soda , preferably cold
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ teaspoon drink mix powder , such as Kool-aid
  • 3 to 4 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Pour club soda, sugar and drink mix powder into a high powered blender.
  2. Add 2 cups ice and blend for 30 seconds.
  3. Add 1 more cup of ice and blend another 30 seconds until desired consistency is reached.
  4. Use up to 1 more cup of ice, if necessary.
