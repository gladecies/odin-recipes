Space Waffles

Background Information

It's like waffles, but in space!

  • Found on Lothal
  • Best eaten with muja sauce.
  • Z'Vk'Thkrkza has a tattoo of a space waffle on her shoulder.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rodian flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 mudhorn eggs
  • 1.5 cups warm bantha milk
  • 0.33 cup blue butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon spice extract

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, baking powder and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Heat cooking device to desired temperature
    • Different species desire different consistency of space waffles.
    • Humans require 375F to cook the batter enough to consume safely.
    • Other species may vary.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in milk, butter and spice.
  4. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture, beat until blended.
  5. Ladle batter into heated cooking device. Cook the waffles to species preference.
