Snowcones!

Description

There aren't many foods colder than a snowcone.

Perfect for sunny days or a cold-induced headache, this recipe from "Loves Bakes Good Cakes" will satisfy any snowcone-consumption desires.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 packet (0.23 oz.) unsweetened drink mix

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan, bring sugar and water to a boil; simmer until the sugar is dissolved, 3 minutes.
  2. Stir in drink mix until well combined.
  3. Remove from the heat and let cool completely.
  4. Transfer syrup to a container for pouring.
  5. To serve, pour the desired amount of syrup over shaved ice to flavor.
